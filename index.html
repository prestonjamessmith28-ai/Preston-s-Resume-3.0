Boundary Waters


    • Every summer my family travels to the North of Minnesota near Ely Minnesota to go to Boundary Waters
    • We start from Low Lake, canoe across the lake, then portage to Bass Lake
    • We set up camp on rock face that gradually leads down to the water
    • We do day hikes around the lake, and always go to the "jacuzzi", which is a nature made small waterfall that goes into a nature jacuzzi
Joy!
